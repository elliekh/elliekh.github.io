Dr. Ellie Hara is a Postdoctoral Research at the Rensselaer Astrobiology Research and Education (RARE) Center. She earned her Ph.D. in Geological Sciences from the University of Colorado Boulder and holds a B.S. in Geology from the University of Southern California. Her research focuses on mineral-facilitated redox reactions and how environmental parameters govern mineral phase transformations. She investigates these processes through experimental geochemistry to understand prebiotically relevant reactions across a range of hydrothermal conditions.
